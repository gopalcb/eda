About Elite Driving Academy (EDA)

Elite Driving Academy (EDA) - is a driving school that has been established in Regina SK, February 2022.

The main services that EDA offers is the techniques for driving light class 5 vehicles to high school students as well as news drivers who carry class 7 learners.

We teach new drivers through 6 & 6 commercial programs. EDA provides services to its clients throughout the year.

Beside 6 & 6 programs; we also offer - Brush-ups service
